# 🎮 NationChain Gameplay Guide

## Overview
NationChain is a blockchain-powered multiplayer strategy game where you become the president of a nation. Manage your economy, build military power, declare wars, and compete with other players in a real-time world.

## Getting Started

### 1. Setup
- Install MetaMask browser extension
- Add Sepolia Testnet to MetaMask
- Get free Sepolia ETH from faucets:
  - https://sepoliafaucet.com/
  - https://www.alchemy.com/faucets/ethereum-sepolia

### 2. Mint Your Country
1. Connect your MetaMask wallet
2. Go to Marketplace
3. Choose a country to mint (costs Sepolia ETH)
4. Wait for transaction confirmation
5. Your country NFT will appear in your wallet

### 3. Find Your Nation
- Your country appears on the World Map with a **GOLD marker** (larger with crown icon 👑)
- Click the gold marker to view your nation's details
- Click "Show Dashboard" to start managing

## Game Features

### 📊 Economy Tab
- **View Resources**: GDP, Oil, Gold, Food, Iron, Uranium
- **Economic Trends**: 30-day GDP chart
- **Resource Management**: Monitor production and consumption

### ⚔️ Military Tab
- **Military Forces**: Infantry, Tanks, Aircraft, Navy, Nuclear
- **Declare War**: Attack other countries
  - Cost: 1000 NATION tokens
  - Duration: 1 hour battle
  - Win probability shown before declaring
- **Active Wars**: Monitor ongoing battles

### 🤝 Diplomacy Tab
- **Alliances**: Form strategic partnerships (coming soon)
- **Trade Proposals**: Exchange resources (coming soon)
- **UN Voting**: Participate in global decisions (coming soon)

### 💰 Treasury Tab
- **Claim GOV**: Daily token rewards
  - Formula: `GDP/80 + Happiness*2 + Oil*1.2`
  - Tokens sent directly to your wallet
  - Can claim once per day
  
- **Stake NATION**: Boost military power
  - 1000 NATION = +10 Military Power
  - Permanent boost to your forces

## Token Economics

### NATION Token
- **Main game currency**
- Earned from: Wars, achievements, gameplay
- Used for: Declaring war, building, trading

### GOV Token
- **Daily utility token**
- Earned from: Daily claims (based on GDP/Happiness/Oil)
- Used for: Construction, research, defense boosts

### Country NFT (ERC-721)
- **Represents your nation**
- Tradeable on marketplace
- Contains all country stats and history

## Strategy Tips

### For Beginners
1. ✅ Choose a country with high GDP (more GOV tokens)
2. ✅ Claim GOV tokens daily
3. ✅ Stake NATION to boost military early
4. ✅ Declare war on weaker countries first

### Advanced Strategies
1. 🎯 Pick countries with high Oil reserves (bonus GOV)
2. 🤝 Form alliances for protection
3. ⏰ Time your wars strategically
4. 💱 Trade resources with other players
5. 📰 Monitor news events for opportunities

## Gameplay Loop

```
1. Claim GOV tokens daily
   ↓
2. Stake NATION to boost military
   ↓
3. Declare war on weaker nations
   ↓
4. Win wars to earn rewards
   ↓
5. Reinvest in military and economy
   ↓
6. Repeat and climb the leaderboard!
```
